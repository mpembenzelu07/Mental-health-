We're Here to Help

Taking the first step toward mental wellness can feel daunting, especially when you're concerned about cultural misunderstanding.

At MindHarmony, we've eliminated that barrier. Every professional in our network has specific training in cultural humility and works with cultural advisors from diverse communities.

Whether you prefer to connect online, by phone, or in person at one of our community-based centers, we'll match you with a professional who understands your cultural context.

We offer services in multiple languages and can accommodate cultural preferences regarding gender of therapist, involvement of family members, and integration of spiritual or traditional practices.
