Mental health materials adapted for different cultural contexts, languages, and belief systems

Resources That Respect Your Culture

Mental health information that makes sense within your cultural framework and values

Standard mental health resources often assume Western individualistic values, nuclear family structures, and specific communication styles. These can feel alienating or irrelevant to people from different cultural backgrounds.

Our culturally-adapted resources are co-created with community members to ensure they resonate with local values, use familiar metaphors, and address culturally-specific stressors.

From mental health guides that incorporate traditional proverbs to self-help materials that respect spiritual beliefs, we provide resources that bridge cultural wisdom with psychological science.
